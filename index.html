MY CAREER
EXPLORATION

SOC ANALYST

A SOC Analyst also known as a Cybersecurity Analyst, monitors an organization’s systems and networks to detect possible cyber threats. This role focuses on reviewing security alerts, analyzing logs, investigating suspicious activities, and supporting incident response.

CORE RESPONSIBILITIES

SOC Analysts monitor alerts from SIEM tools, firewalls, antivirus systems, and IDS/IPS. They investigate phishing emails, malware alerts, suspicious login attempts, and unusual network traffic. They also document findings, prepare incident reports, and escalate serious incidents to senior security teams.

IMPORTANCE OF SOC ANALYST in Cybersecurity

SOC Analysts are important because they act as the first line of defense against cyberattacks. They help organizations detect threats early before they cause serious damage. This role is important for protecting company systems, sensitive data, and daily operations.
